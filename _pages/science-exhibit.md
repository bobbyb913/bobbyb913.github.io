## Overview
I created an interactive prototype of a portable science exhibit to teach about the visual system in our brains. When parts of the optic nerve or optic tract are destroyed, our vision is affected in predictable ways. Depending on where damage occurs on the visual system, it can cause blindness to a single eye or to a "visual field" that is created by both eyes. The portable science exhibit invites learners to put "lesions" along the visual system and hold up the book to their eyes to experience through an altered visual system.
