Hello! My name is Bobby. I am a graduate student at CU Boulder at the ATLAS Institute working towards my master’s degree in Creative Technology and Design. 

I am interested in developing ways people can learn and have fun at the same time. I think that play and games can be powerful tools for learning, and I’d like to develop new tools and exhibits for all ages that teach a wide range of topics.

My background is in neuroscience and biology, so many of my projects are inspired by those fields. In my free time you can find me rock climbing, teaching Italian, and cooking.
